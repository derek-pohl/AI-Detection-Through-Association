How This Works

This tool analyzes text to determine the likelihood that it was generated by AI by comparing the ideas in your text to a freshly AI-generated text on the same topic.

Classifications:

  • Possibly AI: This indicates a high degree of similarity. The number of shared ideas is greater than the number of unique ideas found in both texts combined.
  • Maybe AI: This suggests a moderate similarity. The number of shared ideas is more than 70% of the total unique ideas.
  • Likely not AI: This means the texts are not very similar. The number of shared ideas is 70% or less of the total unique ideas.
